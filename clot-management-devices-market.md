Blood Clotting also known as coagulation is the bodys response to an injury to prevent bleeding. It prevents excessive bleeding when the blood vessels are injured. However, a clot can be harmful when it forms abnormal flows through the vessels. The excessive formation of clots in the blood leads to different diseases such as brain stroke, heart attack and severe leg pain. Blood Clot forms in one or more veins located deep inside a human body would lead to Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) and could damage an organ completely.
