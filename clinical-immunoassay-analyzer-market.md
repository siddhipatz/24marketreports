Immunoassay analyzers perform biochemical tests to detect antibodies that are specific proteins produced by the body to fight antigens. In clinical laboratories, these analyzers are used to detect a variety of substances, including infectious diseases, cardiac markers, endocrine hormone testing, proteins, and for viral or bacterial determination, from a patients biological sample such as blood or body fluid.
